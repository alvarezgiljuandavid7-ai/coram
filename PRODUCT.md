# CorAM Product

## Register

product

## Purpose

CorAM is a web application for church worship teams, singers, musicians and ministry administrators. It centralizes corarios, hymns, courses, resources, user access and admin content management.

## Users

- Singers who need lyrics and chords quickly during rehearsals or services.
- Musicians who need key, tempo and structured song references.
- Ministry leaders who organize repertoire, training and resources.
- Administrators who upload courses, images, videos and documents.

## Product Principles

- Open directly into useful content, not into a prototype explanation.
- Make corarios and hymns searchable and readable first.
- Keep admin actions protected behind real authentication.
- Use a calm, trustworthy interface suitable for repeated ministry work.
- Avoid demo language in user-facing screens.

## Anti References

- Prototype showcase pages.
- Exporter-first developer tools.
- Marketing landing pages that hide the actual app.
- Oversized decorative cards with little operational value.
