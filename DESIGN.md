# CorAM Design

## Theme

CorAM is used by ministry teams before rehearsals, during preparation and after services, often on laptops and phones in bright church offices or warm indoor lighting. The interface should be light, calm and legible.

## Palette

- Primary: deep navy `#0B2545`
- Accent: warm gold `#D4AF37`
- Accent dark: `#B5811F`
- Surface: warm off-white `oklch(99% 0.004 90)`
- Page background: `oklch(98% 0.006 90)`
- Text: slate neutrals with navy headings

## Typography

Use system sans-serif for product clarity. Headings should be bold and compact. Body text should stay readable at normal dashboard density.

## Layout

- Use a real app shell with sidebar navigation and a sticky top bar.
- Prioritize searchable libraries and readable detail panels.
- Keep admin as a protected product section.
- Avoid presenting the mobile simulator as the primary product surface.

## Components

- Primary actions use navy fill.
- Secondary actions use neutral borders.
- Gold marks active content, premium status and ministry emphasis.
- Cards are reserved for repeated content, stats and panels.
